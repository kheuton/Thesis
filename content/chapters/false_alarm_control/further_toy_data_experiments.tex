We tried 2 alternative methods on our toy 2D example from Sec. 5.1. First, we tried a \textbf{weighted cross entropy} classifier, varying the cost of positive examples across 12 log-spaced values from 0.01 to 1000 (See figure \ref{fig:weighted_cross_entropy_toy}).  None of these met the desired precision constraint of 0.9 (the best of these got precision 0.7). Second, we tried a \textbf{log loss} surrogate bound(instead of the hinge in Eban et al's approach). Again, this did not meet our desired 0.9 precision (it got precision=0.72, recall=0.56).
